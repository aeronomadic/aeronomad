Ultra Light UAV

Design-Build-Fly remote sensing drones for water bodies and forest.

Ultra light UAV project at Aeronomad is a fixed wing drone capable of doing remote sensing mission for water bodies and forest. The technological challange of this project is to develop the advance aero-structure airframe to carry much higher payload (Satellites Sensor Suits) during the mission not only that we have to make a GIS software to do the the remote sensing analysis on the aerial data collected by drone. This is very intresting and technologically challenging interdescipliniary project. we want to collect as much data about water bodies & forest to bring new insight about the water & river going through human & other interventions. We are making the documentry while running this program to create awreness. The Ultra light drone program is Physical crowdsourced program where contributor are funding the program to gain work experience while working on this extraordinary project with Aeronomad. The Bakers for Utra Light Drone are the engineering & Science students & organization whom we will be providing work-experience and skills-development program at AeroNomad. We are open for colloboration with scientific organization. Do you have intrest in bird migration? The water bodies? we have mission for you! If you want to be part of extraordinary group of people working to solve the global problem using lastest technological leap then you are welcome aboard! To see the work of your intrest.

AI & Scientific Computing

Make Software for Aero-Projects.
